Partner Relationship Manager (PRM)

The new OFCCP regulations that went into effect March 24, 2014 for VEVRAA and Section 503 require federal contractors to build meaningful relationships with diversity, disability, veteran, female and other minority organizations. This also means recording communications and exchanges with those partners and demonstrating outreach effectiveness.

DirectEmployers Association designed a new tool to help companies streamline this process by providing a single location to enter, access and store this data, regardless of their device or location. This new Member-driven benefit, Partner Relationship Manager (PRM), is included with DirectEmployers Membership at no-additional cost. PRM empowers employers to:

  • Track outreach efforts with local partners
  • Share jobs with relevant partners
  • Record notes on all partner interactions
  • Utilize source code tracking to measure specific partner outreach efforts
  • Pull reports that demonstrate outreach efforts and effectiveness
